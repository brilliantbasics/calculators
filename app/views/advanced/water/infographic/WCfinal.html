Inadequate access to
water and sanitation
affects millions.
783 million people
lack access to an
improved source of
drinking water
2.5 billion people
lack access to an
improved form of
sanitation
To tackle these problems,
8 billion dollars
went into
development for
WASH in 2012




However,
very little money is
budgeted for long-
term maintenance and
financing
Therefore, projects
are prone to failure
For example,
1 in 3 handpumps
are non-functional in Africa
1 in 6 latrines in India are soon
abandoned after construction
Exposing people to unsafe water
sources and open defecation
and the cycle of inequality within the WASH sector remains

services

Daily operations
